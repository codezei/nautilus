What makes your customer support so special? 
We can provide remote support immediately for cloud customers. Our lines remain open via Whatsapp so a more personalised approach is provided every time.  Once a ticket is submitted, our team promises a response time within 24 hrs if not earlier. For on-premise requests, there might be a slight delay given that we have to spend time to travel to the location, or schedule an appointment.
